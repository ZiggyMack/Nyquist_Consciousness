**Description:** The identity manifold (M_p) represents the low-dimensional stable attractor in high-dimensional embedding space where persona samples naturally cluster. Compression operations locate coordinates on this manifold, and reconstruction operators return to the nearest stable basin, preserving identity across transformations.
