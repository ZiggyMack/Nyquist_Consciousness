## Venue Details

### arXiv (No Deadline)

- **Process:** Self-service upload
- **Timeline:** Available 24-48 hours after submission
- **Categories:** cs.AI (primary), cs.LG, cs.CL (cross-list)

### AAAI-26

- **URL:** https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/aaai-26/
- **Location:** Philadelphia
- **Dates:** February 2026
- **Track:** AI Alignment (new in 2025)
- **Format:** 8 pages + references

### NeurIPS 2026 Workshops

- **Multi-Turn Interactions in LLMs:** Best fit for our work
- **Human Feedback for AI Alignment:** Strong fit
- **Submission:** ~August 2026

### Nature Machine Intelligence

- **URL:** https://www.nature.com/natmachintell/
- **Format:** ~3,500 words main text + Methods
- **APC:** £9,190 / $12,690 (open access)
- **Impact Factor:** ~25

### JMLR (Backup)

- **URL:** https://jmlr.org/
- **Format:** No length limit
- **APC:** Free
- **Timeline:** Rolling submissions
