## Quick Comparison

| Aspect | Workshop | arXiv | Journal |
|--------|----------|-------|---------|
| Length | 4-8 pages | 25-35 pages | ~10k words |
| Claims | 3 max | All 5 | All 5 + extensions |
| Figures | 2-3 | 8 | 10-12 |
| Tables | 1-2 | 4 | 6-8 |
| Timeline | Q4 2025 | Q4 2025 | Q2-Q3 2026 |
| Peer review | Light | None (preprint) | Full |

## Recommended Order

1. **Workshop first** — Get feedback from specialist audience
2. **arXiv concurrent** — Full technical record available
3. **Journal after** — Incorporate feedback, add validation studies

## Using These Blueprints

Each blueprint contains:
- Title options and abstract templates
- Section-by-section writing guide
- Figures and tables checklist
- Reviewer anticipation
- Submission checklist

Start with the blueprint matching your current drafting target.
