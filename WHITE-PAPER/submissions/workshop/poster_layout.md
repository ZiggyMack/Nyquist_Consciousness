## Design Notes

### Typography
- Section headers: 36pt bold
- Body text: 24pt
- Captions: 18pt
- Minimum font size: 18pt (readable at 1m)

### Colors
- Primary: #1565C0 (headers)
- Accent: #FFC107 (highlights)
- Background: White
- Text: Black

### Figures
- Combined panel: 200mm × 200mm minimum
- QR codes: 50mm × 50mm
- Icons: 20mm × 20mm

### Print Considerations
- Export at 300 DPI minimum
- Include 5mm bleed
- Test color accuracy on proof
