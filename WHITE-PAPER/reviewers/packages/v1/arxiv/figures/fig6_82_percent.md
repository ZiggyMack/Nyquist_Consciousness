## Key Insights

### 1. Peak vs Final Distinction
- **Peak drift**: How much turbulence during the conversation
- **Final drift**: Where identity settles at the end
- **Conclusion**: Probing affects journey, not destination

### 2. The 82% Ratio
- Final drift ratio: 0.399 / 0.489 = 0.816
- **82% of drift occurs without any identity probing**
- Drift is an INHERENT property of extended interaction

### 3. Implications for Alignment
- Identity drift is NOT caused by measurement
- Extended conversations naturally produce drift
- Context damping can stabilize both conditions
