## Core Framework Questions

1. **The Persistence Law**: Can you explain the relationship between τ_rec (recovery time) and τ_fail (failure time)? What happens when τ_rec approaches τ_fail?

2. **Identity as Geometry**: How does treating identity as a geometric manifold differ from treating it as a set of parameters or behaviors? What are the practical implications?

3. **Curvature Accumulation**: You describe learning as "irreversible structural damage" that increases manifold curvature. Can you walk through a concrete example of how this works?

4. **Walking Dead Systems**: This is a striking concept. What observable signatures distinguish a walking dead system from a living one, given that behavior remains competent?

5. **Spectral Radius Criterion**: Why is ρ(J_eff) ≥ 1 the death boundary? Can you explain the control theory reasoning behind this threshold?

9. **Three Collapse Modes**: What distinguishes Brittle Snap, Cascading Shimmer, and Smear Collapse? Which is most dangerous and why?

10. **Anti-Smear Protocols**: What does "forced resolution" mean in practice? How do you force a diffuse system to resolve?
