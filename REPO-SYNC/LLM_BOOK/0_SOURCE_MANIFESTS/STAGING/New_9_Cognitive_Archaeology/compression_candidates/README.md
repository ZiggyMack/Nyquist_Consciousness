# Compression Candidates

> **Every beautiful mathematical idea begins life in quarantine.**

This directory houses mathematical frameworks that appear to rhyme with what Cognitive Archaeology is building but have not yet satisfied the Third Law. They are applicants, not descriptions.

## What belongs here

Any mathematical framework that someone (human or LLM) proposes as a lens for understanding recovered operators. Each candidate gets its own subdirectory with a standardized `status.md` tracking its promotion history.

## What does NOT belong here

- Adopted frameworks (none yet — and that's correct)
- Vague analogies without testable predictions
- Frameworks that have already failed their predictions

## Third Law (admission requirement for promotion)

> A mathematical framework earns the right to describe the project only after it successfully compresses independently recovered empirical regularities AND makes novel, testable predictions.

Three requirements, all mandatory: (1) compresses, (2) independently recovered, (3) predicts something new.

## Promotion pathway

```
Introduced
  ↓
Unearned Compression Candidate (UCC)
  ↓  predictions registered, tests designed
Tested
  ↓  at least one prediction confirmed, none falsified
Supported Compression Candidate
  ↓  multiple predictions confirmed, compression demonstrated
Earned Compression
```

Each transition is logged in the candidate's `status.md` with date, evidence, and who proposed/tested it.
