# Operator 019: Aesthetic Filtering

**Confidence:** RED
**First recovered:** Dig Site 003 (Dirac)
**Independent confirmations:** 1
**Extractor-independence:** Unknown
**Family:** Aesthetic Generation

---

## Definition

Use mathematical beauty (symmetry, simplicity, structural elegance) as the primary selection criterion for accepting or rejecting a theory — explicitly *overriding* empirical fit when the two conflict.

---

## The Operation

Faced with competing candidates (or a theory that disagrees with data), the thinker evaluates by beauty first and evidence second: a beautiful theory is retained despite contrary data ("the discrepancy will be cleared up later"); an ugly theory is rejected despite empirical success ("a fluke"). Input: candidate theories + an aesthetic standard. Output: an accept/reject verdict. The filter is treated as a *higher-order requirement than experimental agreement* — the "Final Answer" standard.

**Detection condition:** the thinker rejects an empirically-successful result on aesthetic grounds, or defends a beautiful-but-unconfirmed one. Universal-ish among aesthetically-driven thinkers; measurable as an **Aesthetic Threshold** (frequency of empirically-successful rejections) — see EXTRACTION_PROTOCOL v1.3.

**Scope:** Domain-conditional.

---

## Examples

### From Dirac (Dig Site 003)

- "It is more important to have beauty in one's equations than to have them fit experiment."
- **Rejected renormalized QED** despite its staggering empirical accuracy (Lamb shift, electron magnetic moment) as an "ugly" fluke, because it required subtracting infinities.

*Second independent example required for YELLOW.*

## Failure Modes

1. **Beauty's Two Faces:** the same filter that yields triumphs (antimatter, monopoles) yields failures (rejecting successful QED; the Large Numbers Hypothesis). Beauty *generates and selects candidates* but is **not truth-tracking** — it needs an empirical-deference check the operator itself suppresses. (See `DIG_SITES/003_Dirac/excavation.md` §5.)
2. **Founder repudiation:** rejecting the confirmed successes of one's own field on aesthetic grounds, ending in isolation.
