# Operator 009: Contested ≠ Defeated

**Confidence:** RED
**First recovered:** Dig Site 000 (CFA Extractor Calibration — Framework-G transcripts)
**Independent confirmations:** 0 (single source, but 4/4 multi-extractor agreement)
**Extractor-independence:** Partial (2 LLM extractors: Claude, Grok)

---

## Definition

Separate the claim that a position faces genuine difficulty from the stronger claim that it has been refuted — refusing to treat open problems as automatic disqualifiers.

---

## The Operation

A thinker encountering an objection to a position calibrates the damage: does this objection show the position is wrong (defeated), or does it show the position faces a hard problem (contested)? The thinker explicitly refuses to slide from "this is difficult" to "this is dead," maintaining the position as live in the epistemic landscape until the objection actually closes the question.

**Detection condition:** An objection has been raised against a position, and the evaluator (or the thinker themselves) is about to treat the objection as conclusive. The operator fires when there is a gap between the strength of the objection and the strength of the conclusion being drawn from it.

**Scope:** Universal — applies in any evaluative domain. Every field has the distinction between anomaly and falsification, between challenge and defeat.

---

## Examples

### From CFA Deliberation (Dig Site 000)

Claude distinguishes the Problem of Evil creating "tension" in CT's moral architecture from the stronger claim that it renders CT "incoherent." Grok's move from "world-prediction failure" to "incoherent referent" is challenged at exactly this step — the slide from pressure to defeat is not warranted by the evidence.

### Predicted: From Kuhn (Dig Site 011)

Kuhn's distinction between normal and revolutionary science IS this operator at the paradigm level. Anomalies contest a paradigm. Only crisis — accumulated anomalies plus a viable alternative — defeats it. Kuhn's entire framework depends on not treating individual anomalies as falsifications.

### Predicted: From Popper (Dig Site 007)

Popper would appear to violate this operator (any falsification defeats the theory). But sophisticated Popperianism acknowledges that real science doesn't work this way — Lakatos' refinement explicitly distinguishes a "degenerating research program" (contested, losing ground) from a refuted theory (defeated). The operator predicts this refinement.

---

## Failure Modes

1. **Premature Elimination:** Every unresolved difficulty is treated as a refutation. Frameworks with open philosophical problems score zero. The space of live options shrinks artificially because "having a hard problem" is equated with "being wrong."
2. **Damage Inflation:** The epistemic cost of an objection is systematically overstated. A challenge that creates genuine pressure is priced as if it were a decisive refutation. Scoring becomes binary (perfect or dead) rather than calibrated.

---

## Counterexamples

### CE-1: Genuine falsification exists

**Violation:** Sometimes a position really IS defeated, not merely contested. A flat-earther whose position has been decisively falsified is not "contested" — they are wrong.

**Did it work?** Yes — some positions are genuinely defeated, and applying OP-009 to protect them would be motivated reasoning.

**What this tells us:** OP-009 is about **calibration**, not about protecting positions from criticism. The operator says "check whether the objection actually closes the question." Sometimes it does. The operator prevents the default from being "objection = death" — it doesn't prevent genuine falsification from being recognized.

## Alternative Decompositions

### AD-1: Granular damage calibration

**Decomposition:** Contested≠Defeated might be the binary case of a more granular operator: "Calibrate epistemic damage on a scale (objection → anomaly → tension → pressure → challenge → crisis → refutation → defeat)."

**Evidence for:** The CFA extractions already distinguish multiple levels — "tension," "pressure," "contested," "not demonstrated" — suggesting the binary isn't enough.

**Evidence against:** The binary distinction is the load-bearing move. The granular version might decompose into OP-009 (the binary gate) plus a separate calibration heuristic.

**Status:** Open — watch for granular versions in future dig sites.
