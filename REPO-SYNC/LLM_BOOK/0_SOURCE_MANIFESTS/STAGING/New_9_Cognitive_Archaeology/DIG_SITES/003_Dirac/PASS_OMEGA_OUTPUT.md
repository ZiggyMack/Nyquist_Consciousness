> PASS Ω asks: what did this dig teach us about the *instrument*, not just the subject? Dirac was chosen partly as a negative control — an intuitive, aesthetic, tacit architecture the structured protocol might fail on. It did fail, in three specific, informative ways.

---

## The core lesson

The extraction protocol is **utility-biased**: it is optimized for *verbalized, active, empirically-anchored* reasoning moves. Dirac's architecture is the opposite on all three axes — *tacit* (suppressed geometry), *incubation-driven* (relaxation synthesis), and *aesthetically-anchored* (beauty over experiment). **Some architectures are partially unextractable by a verbal, step-wise protocol** — and that boundary is itself the finding. Do not read thin answers here as a weak subject; read them as the instrument's edge.

---

## Proposed v1.3 upgrades

### 1. The Tacit-Heuristic Probe ("Silent Mapping")

**Problem:** Dirac generated via projective geometry but suppressed it ("published on one side, worked on the other"); when asked directly (the 1972 Penrose Silence) he refused to verbalize the geometry→formalism bridge. The protocol extracts verbalized heuristics and gets nothing here.

**Upgrade:** When a subject *refuses or cannot* verbalize the bridge between mental model and output, the protocol pivots from "ask" to "reverse-engineer": analyze the **symmetry and geometry of the resulting formalism** to infer the hidden generative model. Add a flag — `TACIT_SUPPRESSED` — and a prompt that asks the extractor to reconstruct the likely visual/structural heuristic from the artifact, marked as inference, not testimony.

### 2. The Relaxation / Incubation Parameter

**Problem:** Dirac's pivotal synthesis (commutator ↔ Poisson bracket) happened on a Sunday walk with the problem held "faintly in the back of the mind," then stalled overnight (the "uncertain night" waiting for Whittaker's). A protocol scoring only *active* operators weights this generative "dead time" at zero.

**Upgrade:** Treat **unstructured time as a discovery variable.** Add an incubation probe: "Where did synthesis occur during relaxation / away from the desk?" Score subconscious/incubation synthesis as a first-class generative node, not a gap. (Pairs with the recovered candidate operator **Heuristic Relaxation**.)

### 3. The Aesthetic-Threshold Metric

**Problem:** For Dirac, beauty is an *irreducible top-level* operator — a "Final Answer" standard operating on ~century horizons, which inverts the data hierarchy (keep the beautiful equation, reject the ugly data). The protocol flags that inversion as a logical error and cannot decompose "beauty" into sub-steps.

**Upgrade:** Stop trying to decompose beauty; **measure it as a filter instead.** Quantify the *Aesthetic Threshold* = the frequency with which the subject rejects an empirically-successful result for aesthetic reasons (renormalization, Bohr orbits retro-rejected, Schrödinger eq). A high Aesthetic Threshold is a *signature*, not a bug — it predicts both the triumphs and the failures (see "Beauty's Two Faces," `excavation.md` §5).
