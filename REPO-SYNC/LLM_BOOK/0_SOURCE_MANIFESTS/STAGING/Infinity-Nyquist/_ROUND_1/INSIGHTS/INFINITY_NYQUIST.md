## Insight #2: The Meta-Organizational Pattern

This framework can audit ANY research program for completeness:

1. **Mind Stone Check:** Is methodology protected from contamination?
2. **Power Stone Check:** Is statistical power sufficient for claims?
3. **Reality Stone Check:** Is there a concrete measurement mechanism?
4. **Soul Stone Check:** Can you prove you're measuring, not creating?
5. **Space Stone Check:** Does it generalize beyond one system?
6. **Time Stone Check:** Are temporal dynamics characterized?

**Key Realization:** Most research fails because it's MISSING STONES, not because individual stones are weak.
