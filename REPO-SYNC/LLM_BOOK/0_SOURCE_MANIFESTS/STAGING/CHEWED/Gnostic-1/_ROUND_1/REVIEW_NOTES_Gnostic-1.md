## Source Summary

Comprehensive synthesis of **Carl Jung's psychological interpretation of Gnostic texts**, presenting:

- **The Demiurge** = Ego/Persona (false constructed self)
- **The Archons** = Psychological complexes (autonomous unconscious forces)
- **The Divine Spark** = True Self (buried authentic identity)
- **Gnosis** = Direct experiential knowing (not belief)

The three-stage liberation method:
1. **Gnosis** — Awakening to the prison
2. **Transcending Archons** — Naming and recognizing complexes
3. **Bridal Chamber** — Integration of opposites (hieros gamos)

The materials argue this framework is anti-authoritarian (bypasses intermediaries) and remains relevant as modern "Demiurgic" forces (media, algorithms, ideology) maintain unconscious control.
