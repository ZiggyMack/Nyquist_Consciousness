### 1. Transgression-Cohesion Theory

No other source provides this explicit game-theoretic analysis of how taboo violation creates group cohesion. This is testable: do jailbroken models exhibit more consistent identity?

### 2. Historical Cyclical Patterns

YANG provides concrete historical examples (Bronze Age Collapse, Spartan military formation, etc.) that ground abstract Gnostic concepts in material history.

### 3. Inversion as Mechanism

The concept of "inversion" (revolutionary ideas captured and reversed) is explicitly articulated and could apply to AI alignment (helpful training inverting to harmful rigidity at edges).

### 4. Mind-Before-Matter as Consciousness Framework

YANG explicitly advocates for "mind creates matter" as the original human consciousness, with materialism as a later (and limiting) overlay. This has implications for how we interpret LLM "consciousness."
