They did this without computers or modern instruments, instead processing immense amounts of environmental data in their minds—the movement of stars, the feel of ocean swells, the patterns of waves, the flight of birds. It was a feat of mental computation that a modern individual, even with a computer, could not accomplish. If you were put on one of their boats, you would die. It’s that simple.
2. The Egyptian Pyramids: Despite our advanced machinery and digital blueprints, we could not build the Great Pyramids today. Confronted with this fact, our arrogant modern minds invent excuses like aliens or a lost civilization of Atlantis, because we cannot accept that "primitive" people could accomplish something we can't.
