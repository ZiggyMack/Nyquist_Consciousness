## Pre-Mining Hypotheses

### 1. The Hard Problem as Phenomenon B
If local physical/functional descriptions each cohere and pairwise overlaps agree, but no global psychophysical section exists — the explanatory gap is structural, not epistemic.

### 2. CFA as Arrow's Theorem
Pairwise framework agreement failing to produce global ranking may be formally equivalent to Arrow's impossibility theorem in epistemic ordering.

### 3. Operator Composition Obstruction
Cognitive operators extracted locally (per-thinker) may fail to compose into a single global discovery architecture — the Reverse Elephant prediction for Cognitive Archaeology.

### 4. Deflation-Inflation Duality
Phenomenally thick concepts may constitute a precise mechanism: deflating X to property S inflates everything exhibiting S to the status of X.
