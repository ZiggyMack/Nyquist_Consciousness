## Key Themes (Pre-Mining)

### 1. Local-to-Global Extension Failures (Phenomenon A/B/C/D)
The core thesis. Local agreement can: extend uniquely but non-trivially (A), be blocked (B), admit multiple extensions (C), or encounter self-reference (D). Sheaf theory formalizes this.

### 2. Three Senses of Irreducibility
Compositional (no substructure), reflexive (embedded observer), conceptual (circular definition — with sub-types: chain-stops vs opaque). Hidden premises required to move between senses.

### 3. Phenomenally Thick Concepts
Understanding, character, information, measurement, agency, intelligence — concepts whose deflation simultaneously inflates the deflated.

### 4. Averted Vision / Thixotropic Consciousness
Consciousness resists formalization not because it's empty but because investigation changes its state.

### 5. Pre-articulate Intimation
Discovery guided by what you can't yet articulate. Cloudiness preceding the rock of formalization.

### 6. TOE Taxonomy (Types A-E)
Five types of "Theory of Everything" with structurally distinct ambitions, unified by mutual contempt.
