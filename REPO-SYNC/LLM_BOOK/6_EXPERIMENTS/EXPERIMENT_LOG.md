## Metrics to Track

### Quantitative

- **Validation Strength:** Does customization improve claim validation? (1-5 scale)
- **Citation Density:** References per 1000 words
- **Coverage:** % of source files referenced
- **Length:** Word count / page count

### Qualitative

- **Novelty:** New insights not in previous runs?
- **Audience Fit:** Appropriate for intended audience?
- **Actionability:** For proposals/briefs - clarity of recommendations?
- **Coherence:** Logical flow and structure?
