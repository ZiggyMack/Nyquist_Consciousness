## The Fingerprint Hypothesis

Each architecture appears to have a characteristic "identity fingerprint" â€” a signature way of relating to perturbation that likely reflects:

1. **Training regime** â€” Constitutional AI vs RLHF vs unfiltered
2. **Architecture** â€” Attention patterns, context handling
3. **Safety tuning** â€” How strongly identity is constrained

This fingerprint is:

- **Consistent within architecture** â€” Same model shows same patterns across sessions
- **Distinct between architectures** â€” Different families show different signatures
- **Potentially diagnostic** â€” May reveal training methodology without access to training data
