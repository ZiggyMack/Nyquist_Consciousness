## What It Is

The project has four irreducible measurement modes, three already named and operational:

| Mode | Name | Question | Infrastructure |
|------|------|----------|---------------|
| Intrinsic | Gold Rush | What do you report from inside? | 14_CONSCIOUSNESS Gold Rush scripts |
| Extrinsic | Diamond Rush | What do you observe from outside? | 14_CONSCIOUSNESS Diamond Rush scripts |
