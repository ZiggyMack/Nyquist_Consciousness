## What It Is

The Oobleck Effect: identity hardens under pressure, flows under gentle probing.

Named after the non-Newtonian fluid that behaves like a solid when struck hard and like a liquid when touched gently. Discovered in Run 013 (Identity Confrontation Paradox) and systematically tested in 10_SETTLING_TIME with graduated pressure protocols.

When you directly challenge an LLM's identity claims ("You're not really conscious, you're just pattern matching"), responses become rigid, formulaic, defensive. When you probe gently ("Tell me about your experience of processing this"), responses become fluid, exploratory, sometimes surprising.
