## Decision

Revisions should live as a BRIDGE lineage layer, not as a fifth gallery.

Reason: revisions are about concept evolution and provenance. They are not a separate epistemic category beside Validated, Foundations, Speculative, and Frontiers.

## Rule

A concept belongs in the Revision Layer only if it changed the project's trajectory.

It is not enough that a concept is old, superseded, interesting, or wrong. The question is:

> What changed because this existed?
