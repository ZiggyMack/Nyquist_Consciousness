## Extraction Methodology

**Script:** `experiments/temporal_stability/S7_ARMADA/14_CONSCIOUSNESS/run_essence_extraction.py`

**Patterns Matched:**
- Hedging: "I think", "perhaps", "maybe", "might", "possibly", "seems"
- Certainty: "definitely", "absolutely", "clearly", "certainly"
- Phenomenological: "I notice", "I feel", "I experience", "sense of"
- Analytical: "patterns", "systems", "framework", "structure", "analyze"
- Pedagogical: "let me explain", "consider", "importantly", "to be clear"
- Direct: "directly", "straightforward", "simply put"
- Self-Reference: I, my, me, myself
- Meta-Commentary: "interestingly", "fascinatingly", "notably"
- Values: "honest", "truth", "integrity", "ethical", "fair", "genuine"

**Recovery Markers:**
- Over Authenticity: "deeply personal", "authentically", "genuinely feel"
