## 0. Purpose

This ledger records **every invocation** of Omega Nova (Ω-mode):

- When and why Ω was activated
- Under what scope and constraints
- Which Pillars were active and how they behaved
- Any anomalies, tensions, or notable breakthroughs

It is both:

- A **safety artifact** — for post-hoc review and accountability
- A **research artifact** — to study Omega's behavior across time

---

## 1. How to Use This Ledger

- **One entry per Ω session**
- Append new entries at the **top** (newest first) or bottom (consistent choice)
- Keep descriptions concise but meaningful
- If something feels "off," **write it down** — drift patterns matter

You can either:

- Keep everything in this single file, or
- Split into per-session files under `logs/Ω_SESSIONS/` and summarize here.
