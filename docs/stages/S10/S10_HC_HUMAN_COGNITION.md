## Tale's Frame Stack

### The Frame Triple: F(t) = (Fₐ, Fₙ, F_f)

**Aggregated Frame (Fₐ)** — Perceptual Field
- Raw experiential field
- Sensory, affective, bodily states
- Pre-segmented, continuous
- Gibson's affordances live here

**Narrative Frame (Fₙ)** — Story Layer
- Events, sequences, temporal structure
- "What is happening? To whom? In what order?"
- Self-model, memory, identity threads
- Lakoff's metaphors structure this layer

**Factivation Frame (F_f)** — Belief Layer
- Propositions, facts, models
- "What do I take as true? Why?"
- Intent, choice, volition
- Where the Watcher observes from

### Qualia Frame: Q(t) = f(arousal, structure)

The **felt configuration** of:
- Attention placement
- Arousal level (low → high)
- Structural coherence (loose → tight)

Cross-modal inputs (AVLAR/S9) modulate arousal and structure.

## Ego and Watcher Processes

### Ego Process (E)
- Configuration that protects local narrative of "me"
- Manages goals, defense, status, social positioning
- Lives mostly inside **Fₙ** and **F_f**
- **This is what drifts** — not the manifold, not the watcher

### Watcher Process (W)
- Meta-observer that can notice E as an object
- Tracks "this is still me" across frame changes
- Lives as meta-relation over entire F(t)
- **Does not drift** — provides continuity

### Key Insight

> "Identity drift" in AI = Ego drift in humans
> The identity manifold (S5) is stable; the *narrative reconstruction* of it changes.

## Nine Frame Dimensions (Tale)

When a Frame activates, it includes:

1. **Environment** — Physical/situational context
2. **Behaviors** — Actions possible/expected
3. **Capabilities** — Skills, tools, powers
4. **Values & Rules** — Constraints giving shape
5. **Identity** — Who the agent "is" in this frame
6. **Social Strata** — Relationships, roles, hierarchies
7. **Spirit/History** — Cultural background, mythology
8. **Vision/Ideal** — What "good" looks like
9. **Certainty** — Confidence the frame is correct

A frame is activated by **want** (desire). Want switches on attention.
