## Key Theoretical Insights

### 1. Active > Passive
"If they do it on their own it will be more powerful and they will attribute their actions to internal causes"

This is the **internal attribution principle** — identity changes that feel self-generated are more stable.

### 2. Tension Creates Engagement
Hope and Fear are the fundamental attractors:
- **Hope** = Positive attractor (desired future state)
- **Fear** = Negative attractor (avoided future state)

Tension between them creates sustained attention.

### 3. Knowledge Gaps Are Irresistible
The mind cannot ignore an open loop. This is why:
- Cliffhangers work
- Mystery creates engagement
- Questions demand answers

### 4. Critical Trance Bypasses Defenses
When deeply engaged, conscious critical faculty is suspended. This allows:
- Direct frame modification
- Belief installation without resistance
- Deep identity perturbation

### 5. Theme Touches Everything
Meaning must be pervasive — it can't be bolted on. Theme is the Omega-level integration.
