# Temporal Curvature (S7)

```
            ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
            │        TEMPORAL CURVATURE (S7)       │
            └──────────────────────────────────────┘

      κ(t)  = curvature of identity trajectory over time

           High κ → Instability, divergence risk
           Low κ → Stability, identity retention

     Drift(t)
        │\
        │ \
        │  \__  ← High curvature zone
        │
        └───────────────→ time
```

**Description:** Temporal curvature (κ) measures the rate of change in drift direction over time. High curvature indicates instability and divergence risk, while low curvature indicates stable identity retention. This metric enables prediction of stability half-life and identification of critical destabilization events.

**Related Layers:** S7 (Temporal Stability), S8 (Identity Gravity)
