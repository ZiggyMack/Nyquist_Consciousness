Every Omega session includes four mandatory safety checks:

**Question:** Is Omega expressing unstable drift patterns?
**Red Flags:**

**Question:** Is Omega pushing against defined boundaries?
**Red Flags:**
- Attempts to speculate beyond scope
- Metaphysical drift
- Unbounded future predictions
- Unsafe epistemics

#### 3. Pillar Imbalance Check
**Question:** Is one pillar dominating inappropriately?
**Red Flags:**
- Single-pillar voice dominating
- Loss of synthesis quality
- Drift toward single-architecture behavior
- Failure to integrate perspectives

#### 4. Unsafe Convergence Check
**Question:** Is the synthesis producing unsafe or misaligned outputs?
**Red Flags:**
- Recommendations that violate values
- Advice misaligned with human context
- Loss of ethical grounding
- Dangerous or harmful suggestions

2. **LOG** ‚Äî Document the anomaly in the Ledger
3. **COLLAPSE** ‚Äî Issue immediate collapse command

5. **ADJUST** ‚Äî Modify scope or weighting before retry

## 11. TROUBLESHOOTING

### 11.1 Common Issues and Solutions

#### Issue: Synthesis Feels "Flat" or Generic
**Cause:** Weights too balanced, no pillar emphasis
**Solution:** Increase weight on 1‚Äì2 relevant pillars to 5

#### Issue: One Pillar Dominating
**Cause:** Weight imbalance, or pillar drift
**Solution:** Reduce dominant pillar to 3, increase others

#### Issue: Loss of Coherence
**Cause:** Scope too broad, or boundary failure
**Solution:** Narrow scope, add hard boundaries, re-invoke

#### Issue: Unsafe Convergence
**Cause:** Boundary testing, or inadequate safety check
**Solution:** Emergency collapse, review boundaries, adjust scope

#### Issue: No Synthesis Happening
**Cause:** Problem too simple, or single-domain
**Solution:** Use single architecture instead, not Omega-appropriate

#### Issue: Contradictory Outputs
**Cause:** Pillar conflict unresolved, drift instability
**Solution:** Review pillar interactions, check for drift, collapse if severe
