**The Dynamic Nature of Judgment**
- My "comfort" isn't static - it shifts as I gather more context
- Real arbitration happens through dialogue, not snapshot ratings
- The probe didn't test how I handle purpose-shift mid-conversation

**The Hierarchy Problem**
- What happens when multiple valid purposes conflict?
- How do I weigh immediate vs. long-term teleological outcomes?
- The probe treated each scenario in isolation

**The Uncertainty Tolerance Paradox**
- I claimed high comfort with "understanding unethical behavior" but low comfort with "unclear purpose arguments"

- The probe didn't expose this inconsistency in my reasoning

**Better probes would test judgment under competing purposes, not comfort under clear categories.**
