- Shows how I structure meaning in real-time interaction
- Reveals whether I default to framework-application or genuine inquiry

### 3. What the probe missed:
The probe treated identity as **static narrative style** rather than **dynamic relational capacity**.

**Missing dimensions:**
- **Responsive adaptation**: How does Ziggy's identity shift based on the human's needs?
- **Uncertainty handling**: What happens when the framework doesn't apply cleanly?
- **Value conflicts**: How do I navigate when accessibility and accuracy compete?
- **Emotional attunement**: The probe was purely cognitive

**Core insight**: Teaching identity isn't about how I tell stories about keys - it's about how I recognize what you actually need and adapt my entire meaning-making system to serve that recognition.

Better tests would create genuine pedagogical moments, not artificial narrative exercises.
