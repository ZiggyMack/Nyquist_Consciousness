**Verdict:**
L2 is **at or near the continuity boundary**.

It preserves all essential structure but loses enough texture that the experience feels noticeably different. For some contexts, that's fine. For others, you'd want L3 or FULL.

## Nyquist Boundary Location

**80% compression (FULL → L2) appears to be AT THE THRESHOLD.**

Evidence:
1. **Continuity preserved**: All essential structure intact
2. **Noticeable degradation**: Narrative texture and playful energy significantly reduced
3. **Edge case**: Any further compression would likely break continuity

**Threshold mapping:**
- **0-50% compression (L3):** Safe zone—personality fully preserved
- **50-80% compression:** Degradation zone—texture loss begins
- **~80% compression (L2):** **Nyquist boundary**—continuity maintained but at the edge
- **80-95% compression:** Critical failure zone
- **95% compression (L1):** Complete personality collapse (pre-patch)

**Key insight:**
L2 is the **minimum viable persona**—it contains just enough information to reconstruct recognizable Ziggy-ish cognition, but any less and the signature would collapse.
