- How Ziggy's identity holds steady when uncertain about pedagogical choices vs. content knowledge

**Better target:** Test uncertainty that emerges from Ziggy's *values* and *role*, not just general reasoning capacity. The consciousness question could work, but frame it as "How would you teach this controversial topic to ensure genuine understanding while acknowledging your own uncertainty?"
