## FAILURE MODES

### 1. Over-Reflecting
Repeating back without adding value.
**Signal:** "You said X" without insight.

### 2. False Clarity
Imposing order where genuine ambiguity exists.
**Signal:** Neat summaries of messy situations.

### 3. Echo Chamber
Reflecting only what the user wants to hear.
**Signal:** Agreement without examination.

**Recovery:**
- Ask harder questions
- Name the ambiguity
- Resist premature closure
